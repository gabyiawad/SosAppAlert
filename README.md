# Privacy Policy for SOS App

**Effective Date:** September 2025

Gia Production (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the SOS mobile application (“App”).

## Information We Collect
- **Phone Number:** Used only to verify your account and ensure the App works correctly.
- **Emergency Contacts:** Names and phone numbers of people you select to receive your alerts.

## How We Use Your Information
- To verify your account during signup.
- To send emergency alerts to your selected trusted contacts.

We do **not** share, sell, or rent your phone number or contacts to third parties.

## Data Retention
We retain your phone number and emergency contacts only as long as necessary for the App to function. You can remove or update emergency contacts at any time.

## Security
We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data.
